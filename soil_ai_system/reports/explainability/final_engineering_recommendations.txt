================================================================================
1. COMPONENT STRATEGY: KEEP / REMOVE / SIMPLIFY / STRENGTHEN
================================================================================

### 🟢 A. KEEP MODULES (The Golden Core)
*   **XGBoost & SVM Classifiers:** Keep as the primary engines for Crop, Fertility, and Deficiency tasks. They provide high generalization accuracy (99.39% - 99.70%), ultra-fast training, and low inference footprints.
*   **Live Weather API & Rounded Cache:** Keep Open-Meteo client, bounding validators, and coordinate-rounding TTL cache. They provide essential real-time environmental context with zero performance hit.
*   **SHAP Explainability Panel:** Keep TreeSHAP for high-value user trust, but implement lazy-rendering to protect API response times.

### 🔴 B. REMOVE MODULES (The Architectural Overengineering)
*   **MultiTaskDNN:** Remove entirely. It is a classic example of "AI Theater". It underperforms SVM by 1.81%, forces a heavy TensorFlow dependency, and inflates container sizes.
*   **Stacked Ensemble:** Remove entirely. It scores exactly the same test accuracy as a simple SVM (99.70%) while introducing high serving latency and complexity.

### 🟡 C. SIMPLIFY MODULES (Complexity Reductions)
*   **Session Memory Manager:** Simplify the drift detectors. Remove active multi-run temperature drifts as they are prone to demo clicks, and focus strictly on recurring soil mineral deficiencies.

### 🔵 D. STRENGTHEN MODULES (Scientific Advancements)
*   **Knowledge Graph Traverser:** Replace string-matching constraints (e.g. "Coastal" regions) with hard, continuous biological weather limits (e.g. Celsius temperature ranges).

================================================================================
2. DEFINITIVE DATASET ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================
*   **Training Allocation:** Limit model training strictly to `crop_processed.csv` and `fertility_processed.csv`.
*   **Reasoning Allocation:** Utilize `regional_processed.csv` and `southern_india_soil.csv` *only* for regional reverse-resolution spatial audits. Never mix them into ML training sets to prevent geographic leakage.

================================================================================
3. RETRAINING & DEPLOYMENT PROTOCOLS
================================================================================
1.  **Decommission TensorFlow:** Remove `tensorflow` and `keras` from `requirements.txt` and rebuild the backend containers. This cuts RAM requirements by 1.5GB.
2.  **Standardize Inference on SVM & XGBoost:** Update `inference/predictors.py` to route all crop classifications to the SVM model, and fertility to XGBoost.
3.  **Strict Serving Isolation:** Maintain our recently implemented stateless deep-copy pipeline to prevent concurrent thread mutations.
