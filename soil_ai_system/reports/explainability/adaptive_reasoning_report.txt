DETERMINISTIC DECISION RECONSTRUCTION

1. THE PARADIGM SHIFT: RELATIONSHIP-AWARE INFERENCE
---------------------------------------------------
Traditional machine learning yields raw classification probabilities based on
discrete boundaries. Our Relationship-Aware Reasoning upgrade fuses these ML outputs
with:
* Real-time spatial boundaries & climate forecasts (Phase 6Z live context)
* Semantic relationship assertions derived from our Agronomic Knowledge Graph.

The result is a deterministic, explainable, and contextually aware recommendation,
ensuring recommendations never propose crops under hostile localized limits.

2. ADAPTIVE RECOMMENDATION WEIGHTING (DETERMINISTIC RULES)
----------------------------------------------------------
* Module: soil_ai_system/adaptive_reasoning/engine.py
* The engine dynamically adjusts confidence scores based on cumulative timeline
  and session trends, while preserving deterministic and explainable boundaries:
  - Repeated Drought Signals:
    If cumulative weather telemetry in the active session displays a warming trend
    and decreasing air humidity (Drought Trend), any water-thirsty crop (e.g. Rice,
    Sugarcane) suffers an automatic risk penalty (+20.0 to risk offsets), lowering
    its suitability score dynamically.
  - Persistent Nutrient Gaps:
    If consecutive runs detect repeated Nitrogen/Potassium deficiencies (Recurring
    Deficiencies), the engine automatically triggers a CRITICAL RECOVERY state.
    This boosts organic cover cropping, green manure applications, and bio-fertilizer
    schedules to reconstruct the physical soil profile.

3. PRESERVED PIPELINES
----------------------
* All adaptive weightings occur outside the core XGBoost/DNN weights.
* Preprocessing pipelines, standard models, and local SHAP model explainability
  remain entirely pristine, satisfying all critical safety rules.
