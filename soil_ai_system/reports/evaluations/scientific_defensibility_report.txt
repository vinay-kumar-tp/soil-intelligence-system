2. CRITICAL AUDIT OF RULES & MODIFIERS

### A. Nitrogen-Fixer Recuperation (+20 Score Offset)
*   **The Logic:** If Nitrogen is depleted in the soil (<50) and the predicted crop is Groundnut (a legume), the system adds a +20 score offset.
*   **Agronomic Basis:** Extremely sound. Legumes form symbiotic relationships with *Rhizobium* bacteria, fixing atmospheric Nitrogen and converting it to organic forms. Recommending groundnut during Nitrogen gaps acts as a natural, crop-based soil reclamation cycle.

### B. Soil Texture Compatibility (+15 Score Offset / -10 Penalty)
*   **The Logic:** Adding offsets if clay/sandy/loamy matches the crop preference.
*   **Agronomic Basis:** Sound. Soil texture controls water holding capacity and aeration. For example, Rice requires clay/clay-loamy profiles to hold standing water. Planting Rice in sandy soils leads to immediate water percolation and crop failure.

### C. Agro-Climatic Zone Matching (+10 Offset / -5 Penalty)
*   **The Logic:** Checking native region strings (e.g. Coastal, Delta).
*   **Agronomic Basis:** Partially arbitrary.
    *   *The Weakness:* A string match (e.g. "Coastal" vs "Delta") is too coarse. Soil parameters and local temperatures overlap significantly between zones.
    *   *Remediation:* Strengthen this logic by replacing simple zone string matches with hard, continuous biophysical ranges (e.g., matching the crop's actual minimum and maximum thermal and rainfall bounds against real-time API values).

### D. Session Memory Temperature/Rainfall Drift
*   **The Logic:** Adjusting confidence if a "drying trend" (temperature rises $>2^\circ\text{C}$ and humidity falls) is detected over 10 runs.
*   **Agronomic Basis:** Moderately defensible.
    *   *The Weakness:* Tracking drifts across 10 consecutive browser clicks (which happen in a few seconds) is an abstraction of true long-term temporal drifts. It acts nicely as a *demo utility* of session consistency, but has low long-term physical value.
    *   *Remediation:* Limit this to localized village context sessions and clarify in the UI that this represents "active micro-climate session simulation".
