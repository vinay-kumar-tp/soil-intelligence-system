$$\text{Graph Score} = \left(0.35 \times \text{ML Conf}\right) + \left(0.30 \times \text{Env Align}\right) + \left(0.20 \times \text{Graph Compat}\right) + \left(0.15 \times \text{Soil Health}\right) - \text{Risks}$$
