This systems intelligence audit provides a brutal, scientifically defensible,

2. CORE AUDIT CONCLUSION
------------------------
*   The Deep Learning Architecture (MultiTaskDNN) is NOT justified. It underperforms 
    standard tree models (XGBoost) and support vector machines (SVM) while introducing 
    massive TensorFlow memory overhead and latency.
*   The Stacking Ensemble is a classical case of architectural overengineering. It 
    yields a 0.00% accuracy gain over SVM while multiplying serving complexity.
*   Spatial and Weather Intelligence (Phase 6X & 6Z) provide genuine, measurable 
    value by preventing out-of-boundary agricultural suggestions.
*   The Connected Knowledge Graph (Phase 7) is a powerful, deterministic safety 
    harness, but its rules must be strictly grounded to avoid arbitrary weights.
